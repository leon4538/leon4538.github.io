Background of Jesus' character:

Pre-Apocalypse - Grew up in shared housing in Virginia around large numbers of children, generally struggling in forming connections with others.

  • As he grew up and made several friends, he received the nickname "Jesus".
  • Trained in martial arts and became skilled in hand-to-hand combat. Also picking up nifty skills like pick-pocketing and escapology.

Post Apcalypse - Settles in a safe zone called the "Hilltop Colony" after the outbreak.

  • Befriends the community leader, Gregory, and becomes an integral member.
  • Appointed the community's recruiter. His job was to scavenge for supplies and locate other settlements to trade with.
